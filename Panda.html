    Panda

    Store Locations:

    5816 Forbes Ave (Squirrel Hill)
    Add to my favorite stores

    Panda is a Chinese supermarket located in Squirrel Hill that has both traditional groceries (although in limited options) and Chinese imported snacks.

    Panda never fails to surprise me every time I go from its special collection of Lays (cucumber flavor is the best) to its abundance of different ramen options.

    Fun fact: Panda offers a 5% discount to all CMU students. Just show your student ID while checking out.
