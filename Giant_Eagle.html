    Giant Eagle

    Store Locations:

    1901 Murray Ave (Squirrel Hill)
    5550 Centre Ave (Market District)
    Add to my favorite stores

    Giant Eagle (Geagle) has many store locations all across Pittsburgh, the ones closest to campus are on Squirrel Hill and on Centre Ave. They offer a wide variety of fresh produce as well as non-prerishables and daily supplies, covering basically everything you need for your day-to-day life. Giant Eagle Market District is also licensed to sell alcohol (both wine and hard liquor) for those who are interested!

    Fun fact: did you know they are headquartered in Pittsburgh and is not a national brand?
