    Wholey’s

    Store Locations:

    1711 Penn Ave (Strip)
    Add to my favorite stores

    Wholey’s holds a special place in my heart because I love seafood and it specializes in freshly-caught seafoods.

    Wholey’s sell fine seafoods as well as meat, poultry and other fresh produce. It offers curbside pickup and does *nation-wide* shipping as well (impressive, I know.) Wholey’s has a program called weekly in-store specials for in-season seafoods on that week, and there is an in-house seafood diner for customers who want a quick meal.
