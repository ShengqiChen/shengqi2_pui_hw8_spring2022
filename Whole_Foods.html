    Whole Foods

    Store Locations:

    5880 Centre Ave (East Liberty)
    Add to my favorite stores

    Whole Foods specializes healthy, organic, fresh produce that are on the more pricey side. The quality of the foods here are undoubtedly great, and they offer fantastic customer service as well (delivery available with the Amazon app).

    Since they are owned by Amazon, sometimes Whole Foods serve as a return center for Amazon-bought products as well, so it could be convenient to return stuff and pick up groceries at the same spot. Besides their relatively high prices, Whole Foods currently only has one store location in Pittsburgh, which might not be accessible for students living far from East Liberty.
